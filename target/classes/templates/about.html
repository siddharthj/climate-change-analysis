About

Climate change and its impact on our future generations is one of the most discussed topics among environmentalists and people in general. The rate of change has not only been increasing but the ill effects of the same is also evident. In this project we intend to validate if the problem is real and if it is on the rise. If the problem is real, our aim would be to further narrow down the factors responsible for the same. The various factors that are commonly related to the change in climate are:

  • CO 2 emission rates over the years per country.
  • Methane emission rater over the years per country.
  • Nitrous oxide emission rates over the years per country
  • Wilfires over the years
  • Crude Oil, Coal and Natural gas production over the years per country
We are inclined towards getting answers to questions like: Are the above factors` even relevant to climate change? How do they impact our climate? To find answers to these we have tried to enrich the source data by augmenting it with relevant and related data from various sources.
